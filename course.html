  • Level 1 – Helping Teachers to make a discovery of “Self”
    • The objective of the five day programme is to help teachers to explore their inner “self” to become effective persons and creative teachers
    • There will be four sessions of 90 minutes each, each day
    • The sessions will deal with questions of who am I, why am I a teacher, concerns, frustrations, fears, hopes and challenges of the profession, how to be a creative teacher and how to resolve conflicts
    • Dates : First Batch : July 25th to 29th, 2014
    • Second Batch : August 5th to 9th
    • Third Batch : August 19th to 23rd
    • Fourth Batch : August 27th to 31st
  • Level 2 – Self and Others

    • There will be sessions on classroom management, group building, and inter-personal relationship
    • First Batch : 3rd to 7th September 2014
    • Second Batch : 10th to 14th September
    • Third Batch : 22nd to 26th September
