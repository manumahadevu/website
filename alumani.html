Alumni Association Meeting 2018

The Management, Staff and students of St Aloysius Degree College & Post Graduate Courses welcomed the past students on the 6th of October, Saturday from 3 pm - 6.15 pm. At 3 pm refreshments and registration of names was done. At 4.15 pm we all assembled in the auditorium for a formal program. The invocation song was sung by the students choir “You are a Gift” followed by the lighting of the lamp along with Alumni representatives. Fr Vinoo Fabian, the manager and the Vice Principal of the College welcomed the audience. The students put up a dance, a song and a mime on the theme ‘who is an Aloysian.’ A person with human, moral, social and spiritual values, a person for others especially for the poor and the needy. The total number of students present were 64 in number.

After the cultural program business session began in the election of the office bearers for the year 2018 – 2019 After a brief interactive session the following persons were elected for the office bearers posts.

President of St Aloysius Alumni Association is Sunil Kumar (Batch 2009)
Vice President - Arun Stephan
Treasurer - Muthu Kumar
Secretary - Althaf

All the audience congratulated and cheered the office bearers. The solemn Alumni pledge was administered by the Principal Sr Philomena. All the students took the pledge with devotedness. The past students continued interacting with their teaching faculty for sometime after the program. Alumni students were very happy to recall, rejuvenate and reminisce their student days in St Aloysius Degree College.
