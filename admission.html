Admissions

Admissions 2018 - 19

  • As a Catholic College, St. Aloysius Degree College is open to all with a preferential option to the members of the community and the weaker sections. Admission forms will be issued from 1st April 2018. There will be an entrance examination for B.Com./BBA/BCA. The date will be announced on 1st April.
  • The first list of selected candidates will be put up three days after the announcement of Pre-University results of the Karnataka Board. All students for admission have to pass through the interview board.
  • Parents have to accompany the student for interview. All admissions are made by the Principal. Fees will have to be paid within 24 hours of admission.
  • Foreign students and students from outside the state will have special admission fees. Recommendations disqualify candidates.
  • The college does not hire agents for admissions. If any person claims to represent the college and seeks money from any individual for admissions, you are requested to call up the Principal or the President of the college and file a complaint. The case will be handed over to the Police.
  • No donations or capitation fees are charged here.
  • Admission forms are available in the college office from 1st April for Rs 300/-. Students could download them from the website and post them with a cheque of Rs. 350/-. Students who have passed PUC or 12th standards of any board are eligible to apply.
  • Those who have completed their 12th standard could come and meet the Principal from 1st April.

Eligibility for Admission to First Year Degree Course

B.Com./BBA/BCA/BSW/BA

  • Basic eligibility for admission to degree classes is a pass at the +2 level (Karnataka PUC / ISC / CBSE / NIOS / IGCSE / IB). Candidate should have completed 12 years of schooling in 10+2 pattern in any recognized Board in India or abroad
  • “No student shall be eligible for admission to the 1st Degree course through non-formal /distance education unless he has successfully completed 12 years of Schooling through an examination conducted by a Board/University or through National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS)”
  • Candidates who have passed two year PUC examination of Karnataka State with any combination of optional subjects or any other examination recognized as equivalent thereto shall be eligible for admission to BA/BSW courses
  • B.C.A. 1. A pass at the +2 level (Karnataka PUC / ISC / CBSE / NIOS / IGCSE / IB) from any recognized Board in India or abroad. Candidates, who are from non-Science streams, should have studied any of the following subjects at the +2 level: Mathematics, Computer Science or Electronics

Eligibility For Admission To M.Com./MSW

  • A Bachelors degree in BSW/BA of a recognized University with a minimum of 50% marks for MSW. Candidates with work experience will be preferred
  • B.Com / BBM / BBA graduate with minimum of 50% marks can apply for B. Com. Candidates with industry experience will be preferred

Guidelines for Admission

  • Students from outside Bangalore could download the application form and fill the form and send it soon after the 12th Std. results with a DD of Rs. 350/-. You will be informed on the status of the application in 48 hours
  • If selected the applicant has to appear for an interview with the parents
  • The application form should be addressed to: The Principal, St. Aloysius Degree College, Cox Town, Bangalore - 560005
  • A copy of the college prospectus along with the application form will be made available for students from Bangalore at Rs. 300/- from 1st April 2017
  • The applicants could meet the Principal with a copy of SSLC/ I PUC marks soon after with their parents. Those provisionally admitted have to pay the fees in full. (The fees will be refunded if a candidate does not clear the 12th Std. examinations)
  • Two passport size photographs should be submitted
  • SC/ST/OBC students should submit their caste certificate
  • Catholics should attach a letter from the Parish Priest
